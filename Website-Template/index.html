Food

Vegetable Dosa

  • This a batter that is spread on a pan and cooked, the softness of the Dosa depends on how long you cook it and how it is spread. This is a recipe from South India.


Chicken Biryani

  • This is rice cooked with peices of chicken and has a smoky flavour because a container of burning coal is put inside and sealed tightly. This is a recipe from North India

Momos

  • Momos are balls of dough stuffed with vegetables and can be fried or steamed. This recipe is from East India

Vada pav

  • This is basically fried potato between 2 buns called "pav" and the potato part is called "vada", this recipe also includes green chillies

Culture is what we Indians hold close to us, It is our way of life and our right. Culture can be many different things, it could be how we celebrate and why do we do it, it can also be small things like how we talk what rituals we do.

Every month there is always a festival, It can be Diwali(festival of lights) or it could even be Holi(Festival of colors). Because India is a diverse country, there are many releigons with many festivals that are important, this is the reason India has so many holidays. In fact Holi is actually coming next month for 2024!

Temples in India are really sacred as it is a place of worship, it is a rule to remove your shoes before entering so that it does not get dirty. Some old temples have amazing architecture behind them because it would have been difficult to make such huge structures at that time!

In India it is culture to live in joint families, these joint families often include upto three generations most of the time and can vary from a handful of people or more than 10 sometimes!
